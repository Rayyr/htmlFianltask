Popeyes Resturant

Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen , also known as Popeyes and formerly named Popeyes Chicken & Biscuits ,
it is an American multinational chain of fried chicken restaurants formed in 1972 in New Orleans
and headquartered in Miami . It is currently a subsidiary of Toronto-based Restaurant Brands International .
As of 2021, Popeyes has 3,705 restaurants , which are located in more than 46 states and the
District of Columbia , Puerto Rico, and 30 countries worldwide. About 50 locations are company-owned .
